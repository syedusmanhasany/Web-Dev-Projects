TREATMENTS

​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​A healthy mouth, free of infections, injuries and other problems with teeth and gums, is important in maintaining your overall health.

TMJ Treatment

Pain relievers and anti-inflammatories. If over-the-counter pain medications aren't enough to relieve TMJ pain, your doctor or dentist may prescribe stronger pain relievers for a limited time, such as prescription strength ibuprofen..

Implants

They are the screws that imitate the natural tooth root made of titanium, which is accepted as a part of the body placed inside the jaw bone in order to restore the lost function and aesthetics to the cavity of the teeth lost for any reason.

Invisalign

Invisalign is an orthodontic treatment that straightens teeth without the use of metal braces. Invisalign is a series of custom-made clear braces that cover your teeth and gently pull them into the proper position over time.
