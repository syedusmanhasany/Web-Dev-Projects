Dental Hygiene:

Dental hygiene is the practice of keeping our teeth, gums, and mouth clean and healthy. It involves daily habits such as brushing and flossing to remove plaque and prevent tooth decay and gum disease. Regular dental check-ups and cleanings by a dental professional are also important to maintain good oral health. Poor dental hygiene can lead to various dental problems such as cavities, gingivitis, and bad breath. Additionally, recent studies have linked poor dental hygiene to other health issues such as heart disease and diabetes. Therefore, it is essential to maintain good dental hygiene practices to promote overall health and well-being.
